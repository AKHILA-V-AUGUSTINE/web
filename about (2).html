Whats new at AJCE?
AJCE admission 2021 is open for BTech, MTech and MCA courses. Admission and selection process has started for BTech and MCA INT. Admission is likely to close on October 25. Established in 2001, Amal Jyothi College of Engineering (AJCE) is a private college located in Kottayam. AJCE is affiliated to Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) and approved by the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE). It is the first college to be accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for primary departments. The college is also accredited grade ‘A’ by NAAC under the UGC, Government of India. AJCE has 13 departments offering 34 courses across four streams at UG, PG, and PhD level.

Besides, add-on courses like GATE coaching, foreign language courses, CAD training etc., are also offered by AJCE. The college has tie-ups with 49 companies and institutes, namely Red Hat India Pvt Ltd., Royal Enfield, IIT Bombay, etc. Department of Placement and Training organises a placement drive every year for students of different departments.
